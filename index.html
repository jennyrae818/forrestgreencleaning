About Me

I was born and raised in Edmonds, WA and now reside in Tacoma, WA with my husband and two mini austrailian shepards.

After high school, my career started quickly as a Client Associate at Merrill Lynch in Seattle, WA. I relocated to Century City, CA and spent the next 9 years with the brokerage firm.

I was ready for a new challenge and a change of scenery. The next year, I walked across the country of Spain over the span of 45 days. Link of "the way": The Camino De Santiago

Since 2019, I have proudly been servicing my community as a Fire Dispatcher for Pierce County.

In 2021, I started a small house cleaning business named Forrest Green Cleaning, LLC.

I am best described as an organized, curious minded, creative, helper.

My goal is to help others through web development!
